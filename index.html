Shapes of three different colors 🔴 ⚪ 🔵 approach the center of the screen. Ensure they are the same color when they meet!

How to play: TAP any shapes on the tracks to ↪️swap↩️ it with the shape before it. Once you've made a match, the shapes zoom towards the center!



Strategize: Matching the same color in a row grants you chain points. Line up your shapes to score some extra points! 🔴 🔴 🔴 ➡️ ⬅️ 🔴 🔴 🔴

Unlock New Themes: Get 💲coins💲 for every chain point, and unlock new shapes and color schemes! 🔵 🔻 ⬛



Track your Stats: Compete with your friends to see who can get the high score ⭐!
Mirror Match saves your top 10 high scores, each by name. Take turns on the same device!

Take it up a notch: The game feel too easy? Get straight into the action with Expert Mode, and begin at MAX difficulty! 😱



Keep track of:
🔻How many games you've played
🔻High many shapes you've matched
🔻And your chain point percentage (how strategic are you?? 🤔)
